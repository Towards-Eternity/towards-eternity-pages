Who We Are?

Where Did This Journey Reaching Millions Begin?

Year 2010... A few university students came together for a purpose. They wanted to do something for the young people of the ummah who were melting and hurting in the face of sins. They shared their concerns... They shared their concerns... They shared their concerns... There had to be a way to touch the hearts of millions. To make their voices heard...

Finding a Way to Spread the Word of Allah Everywhere There had to be a way for the prayers of mothers to be answered for their children. Their troubles gave strength to their prayers. A journey that began with a small camera and microphone became a YouTube channel with 3 million subscribers.

A Small Camera and Recording!

With sincere, sympathetic, and sometimes impressive speeches in the language of young people, thousands of lives have changed. Some were led to prayer... Some were led to wearing the hijab... Some were led to abandon unlawful deeds. Through an internet cable, Allah and His Messenger (peace be upon him) were introduced into millions of homes.
